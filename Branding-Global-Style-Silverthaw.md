### **Design System & Global Styles**

* **Primary Font:** Playfair Display (Use for all Section Headings, Hero Headline, and large emphasis text to establish authority).  
* **Secondary Font:** Open Sans (Use for all body copy, bullet points, and button text for high readability).  
* **Primary Colour:** `#022549` (Deep Blue \- Use for Hero background, primary Call-To-Action buttons, and primary text).  
* **Secondary Colour:** `#c0c0c0` (Silver/Grey \- Use for alternating section backgrounds, subtle borders, or secondary button hover states).  
* **Brand Asset:** Place Silver-thaw-logo.jpg in the top-left navigation bar and the footer.
